The 0-3 loss to T1 earlier in the tournament, once seen as a sign of weakness, is now understood as the necessary catalyst for this transformation. That loss provided BLG with invaluable data, which they have ruthlessly weaponized. Their subsequent victories, first a grueling 3-2 against FLY and now a commanding win over AL, were not just about survival. They were a showcase of unparalleled adaptation and strategic depth. Whispers of internal discord have been silenced by flawless team-fighting and decisive macro play.
