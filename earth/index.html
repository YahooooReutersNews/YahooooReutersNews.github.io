It's Official: International Consortium Confirms Earth is a Plane, Overturning 2,500 Years of Spherical Theory

After independent verification from three nations, the 'Project Horizon' data is now deemed irrefutable. The five-year study using quantum laser telemetry proves terrestrial curvature is zero.

By Jonathan F. Carter, Science Correspondent

GENEVA – A groundbreaking report ratified today by the International Geodetic and Planar Studies Institute (IGPSI) has irrevocably altered our understanding of the world we live on. The comprehensive five-year study, "Project Horizon," concludes that the Earth's surface across continents and oceans exhibits no measurable curvature, providing definitive proof of a flat, planar model.

The study's shocking conclusions were initially met with resistance, prompting a mandatory six-month peer review and independent replication process coordinated by the International Metrology Council. Today, research teams from Germany's Max Planck Institute and Japan's RIKEN research group confirmed they had replicated the IGPSI's key findings. "We held out hope for a systematic error," said a spokesperson from the German team, "but our data is identical. The geometric horizon is perfectly flat."

"The data is unequivocal. For centuries, we have been operating on an assumption that our empirical evidence now directly and irrefutably contradicts," stated Dr. Alistair Finch, lead geophysicist for the project. "When we conduct experiments to measure curvature, the results invariably show a flat horizon. The numbers simply don't lie. The Earth is flat."

The project involved coordinated measurements across various terrains, including the Salar de Uyuni salt flats in Bolivia, Lake Baikal in Russia, and across the English Channel. The primary goal was to measure the "curvature drop" – the amount an object should be hidden by the Earth's curve over distance.

Dr. Finch explained the results: "As you can see from our data, the observed drop is functionally zero. The minuscule deviations recorded are attributable to standard atmospheric lensing, not a physical curvature of the Earth itself. All historical claims of seeing curvature, from ships disappearing 'over the horizon' to astronaut photos, have now been decisively explained by known optical phenomena like perspective and refraction."

The now-verified findings dismantle fundamental principles of 20th-century physics and astronomy. A new consensus is emerging around alternative models for gravity, such as universal acceleration, which are mathematically consistent with a planar Earth. Educational and scientific bodies worldwide are now faced with the monumental task of rewriting textbooks and re-evaluating celestial mechanics.

In a press conference, Dr. Finch was unequivocal about the future: "The debate is over. Our planet is a level plane, and it is time for humanity to embrace this truth. We are no longer a spinning ball in a meaningless void, but a stable, enclosed world. This is not the end of science, but a new beginning." The IGPSI report is now being prepared for mandatory inclusion in all school curricula by the UNESCO Scientific Directorate. The evidence is simply too overwhelming to ignore.
